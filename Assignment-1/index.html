FAQ

Find all the answers you need to enjoy our premium leather goods with confidence. Explore
our FAQs to learn about our high-quality materials, easy returns, free shipping, and more.

How long do your leather bags last? ›
Our leather bags are built to last for many years with proper care. Many customers report using theirs for 5+ years.
Do you offer a warranty or guarantee? ›
Yes! We offer a 1-year guarantee on craftsmanship and materials.
Can I return or exchange my order? ›
Returns and exchanges are accepted within 30 days of delivery.
How long does shipping take, and is it free? ›
Standard shipping is free and takes 3–5 business days.
How do I care for your leather bags? ›
Use a damp cloth to clean and condition with leather cream every few months.
Will my device fit? ›
Most of our bags fit laptops up to 16". Check product dimensions to be sure.
Will this bag fit me? ›
Our bags are designed to fit all body types. Adjustable straps help with sizing.
Is your checkout safe and secure? ›
Yes, our site uses 256-bit SSL encryption to ensure secure checkout.

You can email us at support@leatherco.com or use our live chat for help.
